CoachPitch Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

CoachPitch respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CoachPitch handles information in connection with the CoachPitch mobile and wearable applications and the CoachPitch website.

1. Information the CoachPitch app collects

The CoachPitch app is designed so that it does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from users through the app itself.

CoachPitch does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, location data, contacts, photos, payment information, health information, or advertising identifiers through the app.

2. Analytics and tracking

CoachPitch does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the app to track users across apps or websites.

3. Account creation

The CoachPitch app does not require users to create an account in order to use the app's core functionality.

4. Beta signup and website forms

If you voluntarily submit your email address or other contact information through the CoachPitch website, including a beta signup or contact form, that information may be used solely to respond to your request, provide beta-related updates, or share information about CoachPitch.

Information you submit through the website is collected only if you choose to provide it. It is not sold to third parties.

5. Third-party services

The CoachPitch website may use third-party services to host the website, process beta signup forms, or deliver email communications. Those services may process information you voluntarily submit to them, subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Children's privacy

CoachPitch is not intended to collect personal information from children. The app itself does not collect personal information. If you believe information was submitted through the website by a child without appropriate permission, please contact CoachPitch so it can be removed.

7. Data retention

Because the CoachPitch app does not collect personal information, there is no in-app personal data retained by CoachPitch. If you submit contact information through the website, that information may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry, manage beta communications, or comply with legal obligations.

8. Security

CoachPitch takes reasonable measures to protect information voluntarily submitted through the website. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

9. Your choices

You are not required to provide personal information to use the CoachPitch app. If you have submitted your email address through the website and would like it removed from future communications, you may request deletion by contacting CoachPitch.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact CoachPitch at:

hello@coachpitch.app
