Frequently asked questions

What is CoachPitch?

CoachPitch is an app that uses Apple and Android/WearOS devices to send and receive pitch calls and other signals from coach to player.

How does CoachPitch work?

CoachPitch will send pitch calls from the phone app (iPhone/Android) to bluetooth paired watch devices (Apple Watch/WearOS).

Is it legal?

Electronic communication has been approved in almost all sanctions of softball and baseball. In many cases, the coach is only allowed to use electronic methods to communicate with the catcher on defense only. Nevertheless, it's always recommended to confirm legality with the tournament director or UIC.

Does CoachPitch work for softball and baseball?

Yes, pitch calls can be configured to different types of pitches specific to each sport.

When will CoachPitch be available?

Stay tuned for early access release of CoachPitch, which will be available on Apple App Store and Google Play.

How much will CoachPitch cost?

CoachPitch Plus, which includes all existing functionality, will be available for free in the early access period. Once the early access period ends, CoachPitch Plus will be available for new users for a one time fee, while a free version of CoachPitch will be made available for download.
